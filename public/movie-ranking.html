Flo's Best Movies in 2024

My top 3 movies of all time.

Gifted Hands

This is an inspirational and educative movie on the life of Dr Ben Carson,
a neurosurgeon who was raised by a single mother and faced many difficulties
in his childhood, faces a challenge to operate two Siamese twins linked by the skull.

Fighting Temptation

The plot revolves around Darrin Hill who was overwhelmed with huge debts and the thought of no job.
His only hope is a surprise inheritance worth a fortune. However, in order to get this fortune,
he must first get his hometown choir in shipshape condition which happens to be a huge task.

Trading Places

A snobbish investor and a wily street con artist find their positions reversed as part of a bet by two
obnoxious millionaires. These millionaires are the rich Duke brothers who are determined to see
whether heredity or the environment impact a person's well-being. They swap the lives of a poor
street hustler and an employed aristocrat for a ridiculous amount.
